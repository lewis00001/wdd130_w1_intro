About Me: Lewis Lockhart

Early Life

My early life is hard to explain but I will give it a try. My conception was part of a program called Project Mendacium and was a remnant of the Carter Administration allowed to continue when President Reagan took office. I was one of nearly 700 original attempts to create human life within a laboratory womb (called Mother Genesis). Just my sister Ocean and I were successful. I struggle with the idea of genealogy work since I have none to speak of.

Education / Career

I have only been 'out in the real world' for 15 years now. I have been allowed to attend college but am required to get two separate degrees. The first one was to be a MFA in conceptual art and the second is required to be in a technical field such as software engineering, which is what I study now. Currently I work software test engineer at Simplifile which is a company that works with banks, title companies, and county offices to streamline the mortgage process. We are located by the Provo Riverwoods. 

Hobbies

I have interest and proficiency in the following areas: history, mechanical design, music, cooking, motion graphics, drawing, writing (short stories / comedy), management, programming, business analytics and accounting.
